Weather and Crime

These two chats represent the proportion of each type of the weathers and the sum of all crimes happened under each weather condition respectively. The “fair” weather takes up the most percentage of all weather types, and the crime happen the most in the “fair” weather as well.

This chart represents the number of crimes in each range of temperature based on each type of crime. Crimes happened mostly between 50 to 70 Fahrenheit degrees.
